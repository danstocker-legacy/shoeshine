<static> create(eventName, eventSpace) → {shoeshine.WidgetEvent}

Creates a WidgetEvent instance. Do not instantiate this class directly. Spawn events on the event space `shoeshine.widgetEventSpace`, or an Evented instance, like a Widget. WidgetEvent may also be instantiated by creating an `evan.Event` with `shoeshine.WidgetEventSpace` specified as event space.
